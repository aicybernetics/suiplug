About SuiPlug

Abstract

Suiplug is a Web3-based e-commerce platform that offers a seamless, decentralized shopping experience for high-quality gadgets, including phones and laptops. Built on the Sui blockchain, Suiplug leverages smart contracts, NFT ownership, and cryptocurrency payments to provide secure, transparent, and efficient transactions, creating a new paradigm in online shopping.
